# Comprehensive Analysis Framework

## 1. Technical Analysis Engine
- **Price Patterns**: Head & shoulders, triangles, flags using TA-Lib
- **Indicators**: RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Fibonacci retracements
- **Volume Analysis**: OBV, Volume Profile, Accumulation/Distribution
- **Market Structure**: Support/resistance, trend lines, channels
- **Advanced**: Elliott Wave, Ichimoku Cloud, Market Profile

## 2. Fundamental Analysis Engine
- **Financial Ratios**: P/E, PEG, P/B, P/S, EV/EBITDA (calculated from SEC filings)
- **Growth Metrics**: Revenue growth, earnings growth, margin expansion
- **Quality Metrics**: ROE, ROIC, FCF yield, debt/equity
- **Valuation Models**: DCF, DDM, Residual Income, Sum-of-parts
- **Peer Comparison**: Industry-relative metrics, percentile rankings

## 3. Sentiment Analysis Engine
- **News Sentiment**: FinBERT on financial news (weighted by source credibility)
- **Social Sentiment**: Reddit WSB, Twitter, StockTwits analysis
- **Insider Activity**: Form 4 filings analysis
- **Analyst Sentiment**: Consensus ratings, estimate revisions
- **Options Flow**: Put/call ratios, unusual options activity

## 4. Macro/Geopolitical Analysis
- **Economic Indicators**: GDP, inflation, employment (FRED API)
- **Sector Rotation**: Business cycle positioning
- **Currency Impact**: DXY correlation analysis
- **Commodity Exposure**: Oil, gold, materials sensitivity
- **Regulatory Risk**: SEC filing keyword analysis, policy trackers

## 5. Alternative Data Integration
- **Web Traffic**: Google Trends for company products/services
- **Weather Impact**: Sector-specific weather correlations
- **Satellite Data**: Parking lot analysis, crop yields
- **Supply Chain**: Shipping data, port activity
- **ESG Factors**: Environmental scores, governance metrics

## 6. Risk Management Layer
- **Portfolio Risk**: VaR, CVaR, Sharpe ratio optimization
- **Correlation Analysis**: Sector/market correlations
- **Stress Testing**: Monte Carlo simulations
- **Liquidity Risk**: Bid-ask spreads, volume analysis
- **Black Swan Detection**: Tail risk indicators

## 7. Moat & Competitive Analysis
- **Competitive Positioning**: Market share trends, pricing power
- **Moat Indicators**: Gross margins, customer retention, network effects
- **Management Quality**: Insider ownership, capital allocation history
- **Innovation Metrics**: R&D efficiency, patent analysis
- **Brand Strength**: Social media mentions, customer reviews
